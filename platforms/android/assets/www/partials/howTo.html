How To

  • Play the first combination the app gives you.
  • A {{rightPlaceColour()}} peg means there is a correct colour on a correct place. A white peg means there is a correct colour, but on the wrong place.
  • Enter the pegs that are returned.
  • Tap "Submit" to get the next combination to play.
  • If you entered something wrong, tap "Undo" to go back.
  • Tap "Reset" to start again

Options

  • High Accuracy: this decreases the maximum amount of guesses at the cost of performance.
  • Allow duplicates: toggles duplicate colours in the combinations.
  • Random start combination: will generate a random start combination.
  • Right Place Colour: sets the colour to signify the right colour is at the right place.
